Wholistic Tracking

The Sheet captures all parts of your financial position (Stocks, ETF’s, Managed Funds, Dividends, Super etc).

Live Prices

Live prices for ETFs, Stocks, Crypto & Managed Fund prices with inbuilt Dividend Tracking.

Algorithmic Investing

The Sheet automatically optimizes when & what ETF’s to rebalance into, setting a Calender invite and emailing you when it's time.

Monthly Reporting

The Sheet tracks your monthly Networth for all asset classes and emails you a monthly progress report

Autmatic Allocations

The inbuilt budgetting Sheet guides your investment/savings amounts and calculates monthly savings transfers.

The Future

The Sheet tracks and records all side income & retirement/super amounts

Loan/Debt Support

The Sheet will help you track and capture progress made on Loan/Debt payments

Your Property

The Sheet will track and feed into your total Networth your Property value
